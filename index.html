Frequently Asked Questions

Why can I only play once per day?

Each app is designed as a daily challenge — the same five questions go out to every player at midnight. One shot keeps it fair and the streak meaningful.

My streak disappeared — what happened?

Streaks require playing on consecutive calendar days. If you miss a day, the streak resets. Make sure to play before midnight in your local time zone.

The app isn't loading / crashing on launch.

Try closing the app fully and reopening it. If the issue persists, delete and reinstall the app — your streak and history are stored locally but a reinstall will reset them. Contact us if the problem continues.

I think a question or answer is wrong.

We take accuracy seriously. Please email us with the question text and the app name and we'll review it right away.

How do I share my score?

After completing the daily quiz, tap Share Results on the results screen. This copies your emoji score card and opens the iOS share sheet so you can send it anywhere.

Are there ads or in-app purchases?

No ads, no in-app purchases, no subscriptions. All three apps are completely free.

What data does the app collect?

Nothing. All data (streaks, scores, history) is stored locally on your device only. No accounts, no tracking, no servers.
