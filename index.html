Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Our apps do not require you to create an account or provide any personal details to use them.

Data Stored on Your Device

To provide core app functionality, the following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Your daily quiz answers and scores
  • Your current and best game streaks
  • Your game history (up to the last 30 games)

This data is stored using Apple's standard on-device storage (AsyncStorage) and remains entirely under your control. It is removed when you uninstall the app.

Audio

Our apps play short sound effects in response to correct and incorrect answers. The apps do not record, access, or use your device's microphone at any time.

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services. No data is shared with any third party.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected from any user, these apps are safe for all ages.
